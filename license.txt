Compliance Notice

This repository collects, processes, and visualizes ONLY public information from openly accessible sources. No login is used; no paywalls are bypassed; no personal sensitive data (e.g., ID numbers, phone numbers) is collected.

Crawler rules
- Always respect robots.txt of each domain. If robots disallow or cannot be fetched, the crawler will not access that domain.
- Apply per-domain rate limits (RPM) and exponential backoff on 429/5xx with Retry-After.
- Record request logs and errors for auditing.

Data usage
- Public data is used for research/analytics demonstration only.
- Please follow the original sources' terms of use and attribution.

Attribution
- World Bank Open Data (api.worldbank.org/v2) under CC BY 4.0, see: https://data.worldbank.org/summary
- Government websites content remains the property of original owners.

Security & Privacy
- No attempts to de-obfuscate, circumvent security controls, or collect PII.

Contact
- For takedown or questions, please open an issue in the repository.
